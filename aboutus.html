About Us: Finster is a website created to help people plan outings with their friends and family in an easy and organized environment. To make it easy and accessible for friends and/or family who you want to invite, we let you create your own account. Once you log in, you can search for any location in the world via our Google Map. Once you pick a location, it’ll take you to the voting page in which your friends and family can vote for whether or not they’d like to hang out there with you.

The coders for Finster are four teenagers from Atlanta, Georgia; Alicia Silverio, Dhyuti Gopalakrishna, Mena Sengupta, and Carrington Parks. The group was formed with the help of Girls Who Code, a free Summer Immersion Program formed to get more females into the technology industry. It is a seven-week program. They learned multiple coding languages within the program - some of which include: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and more, all of which they used to create Finster.
